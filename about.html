THE STORY

Delight Linux is a custom OS built from scratch on a Fire Tablet 8 (12th Gen). While most people use tablets for watching videos, we used ours to compile a new future.

Our Mission

Determination: Never giving up, even when the compiler throws 1,000 errors.

Performance: Squeezing every bit of power out of the hardware.

Freedom: A system that belongs to the user, not a corporation.
